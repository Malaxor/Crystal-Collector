Crystals Collector

Next to "Catch me:", a random number will be displayed at the start of every new game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to you total score.

You win by matching your total score to the random number; you lose if your total score surpasses the random number.

The value (points) of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on a crystal.

At the start of every new game, each crystal will be alloted a new random value (points).
